Searching tips

____________________________

Lunar.js useful searching features:

Feature Syntax What it does
Fuzzy Match "clone~1" Finds "cloned" or "cone" (allows 1 typo).
Wildcard "hardw*" Finds "hardware", "hardworking", etc.
Required "+SSD" The result must contain the word SSD.
Exclude "-HDD" The result must not contain the word HDD.
Boosts "DDR5^2 DDR4" Weights the term "DDR5" 2 times higher than the term "DDR4"
